Support the Nu Charge

Donations through this site will be utilized by the Nu Charge in order to help support our twice annual philanthropies, necessary housing maintenance and educational activities. Feel free to donate any amount that you wish, all donations are greatly appreciated. Some popular amounts include, $18.57 and $18.11. Please consult a professional tax advisor on the deductibility of any donations.

Our current philanthropy commitments include a partnership with Autism Speaks at the University of Virginia. This year brothers will be participating in conjunction with members of Autism Speaks in a 5K walk/run in Richmond, VA. Last year, we also conducted the first annual Theta-Delt-a-Thon music festival. In the coming year, we hope to expand on that traditional by increasing attendance and band participation.

Our fraternity house is one of historic and illustrious tradition. With such a rich history, new maintenance projects are a constant concern. Current projects include plans to re-tile the basement, replace aging couches in our main room and replace appliances in our house kitchen. These projects will increase the comfort of both brothers and guests of the Nu charge.

We currently hold one major educational goal in mind. At the moment, the house's charge room is used as a general storage area by those living in the house. Our goal is to convert this space into a study room of some variety. This will further our commitment to academic excellence and will create a quiet space of for brothers living in the house.

If you would like more information regarding how donations are allocated or would like to specify your donation to a specific purpose please contact our President or Treasurer. Contact information for our executive board can be found on the 'Contact Us' page above. Once again, thank you for supporting the Nu Charge.
