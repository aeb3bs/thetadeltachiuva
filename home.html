Theta Delta Chi

Welcome to the official homepage of Theta Delta Chi at The University of Virginia. Since 1857, the Nu Charge of Theta Delta Chi has upheld some of the oldest fraternal bonds at the university. We are dedicated to serving the community, and forming a lasting and meaningful brotherhood.

The purpose of this website is to not only establish ourselves, but keep our Alumni up to date with fraternal business. Upcoming alumni events will be posted here on the home page if you are interested.

Feel free to contact the alumni chair regarding questions about the website or other business matters.
